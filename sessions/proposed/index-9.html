Proposed Sessions

The time for one session is maximum 60 minutes. The first 45 minutes are for the session itself, while the next 15 mins are for attendees' questions. When the session lasts too long, it must be stopped after 60 minutes to allow the participants to go to the next lecture and the speakers to prepare for the next presentation
